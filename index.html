Step 1: Sear the chicken until golden brown in the pan with the salt and pepper. Take out and set aside.

Step 2: In the same pan, fry the garlic and red pepper for a couple of minutes before stirring in the soy sauce, ketchup, and vinegar.

Step 3: Add the chicken and noodles, and mix together for another couple of minutes.

Step 4: Garnish with sesame seeds.

Step 5: Enjoy!

Recipe #2: 3-Ingredient Chili-glazed Salmon

  • Time: Less than 30 minutes
  • Serves: 3
IngredientsQuantity
salmon,fillets
chili sauce
fresh scallions, chopped
4 oz, 3 (115 g)
½ cup (130 g) (25 g)
¼ cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 400°F (200˚C).

Step 2: In a bowl, mix together the salmon, chili sauce, and the scallions.

Step 3: Place the fillets on a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Spoon any leftover sauce on top of the salmon.

Step 4: Bake for 12-15 minutes, until the salmon is cooked but still tender.

Step 5: Enjoy!

Recipe #3: 3-ingredient Peanut Butter Cups

  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
powdered sugar, sifted
creamy peanut butter
chocolate, melted
3 tablespoons
½ cup
1 cup

Step 1: Prepare a cupcake tin with 6 liners.

Step 2: Stir peanut butter and powdered sugar together until smooth.

Step 3: Spread 1 to 2 tablespoons of chocolate in the bottom of each cupcake liner.

Step 4: Dollop 1 to 2 teaspoons of the peanut butter mixture on top of the chocolate.

Step 5: Cover each dollop of peanut butter with more chocolate and smooth out the top.

Step 6: Refrigerate for 1 hour or until chocolate has hardened.

Step 7: Remove peanut butter cups from the liners.

Step 8: Enjoy!
